Momo

Description

Momo is a popular Nepali and Tibetan dumpling, filled with seasoned meat or vegetables and steamed to perfection. These dumplings are juicy, flavorful, and served with a spicy dipping sauce. They are a favorite street food and homemade delicacy enjoyed across South Asia.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup water (as needed)
  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour with water to form a soft dough. Knead for 5 minutes and let it rest.
  3. In another bowl, mix ground chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, black pepper, salt, green onions, and sesame oil.
  4. Roll out small circles of dough and place a spoonful of filling in the center.
  5. Fold and pleat the edges to seal the dumplings.
  6. Steam the momos for 12-15 minutes until fully cooked.
  7. Serve hot with a spicy dipping sauce.
