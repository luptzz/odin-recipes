Burger

Description

This classic beef burger recipe features a juicy patty grilled to perfection, topped with cheese, fresh lettuce, and tomato, all inside a toasted bun. It’s simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Form into 4 equal patties.
  4. Grill each side for 3-4 minutes, adding cheese on top near the end.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  6. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on the buns.
  7. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, and patty.
  8. Serve immediately.
